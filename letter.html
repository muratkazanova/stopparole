On May 12, 2017, your predecessor, Jerry Brown, reversed the BPH’s similarly misguided decision of December 14, 2017, to release Sassounian. I respectfully ask that you kindly do the same, and that you ask the BPH to give Sassounian a denial of longer than three years.
